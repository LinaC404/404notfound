Why I want to continue study

When I was a kid, my dream was to be a scientist.
Maybe the reason is to be a scientist always sounds cool.

I didn't know the weight of my dream and forgot what I want to be with my growth.
I graduated and worked like most guys in the world.

Gradually, I felt that something was not right, just like I didn't feel like I'm living in the real world when I wake up.

And now here is my secret, a very simple secret: It is only with the heart that one can see rightly; what is essential is invisible to the eye.

I realized people must love the one they love, went at what they love hammer and tongs.

I like the book The little prince.

It is the time you have wasted for your rose that makes your rose so important.

My rose is my dream.

This time, I want to use data to create something fun!
